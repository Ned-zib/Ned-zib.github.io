What I’m good at?

I am great at thinking in code and solutions, finding the best way to make an action, and performing it. I am trying to be the best Full stack dev and a device inventor, I would like to change the world for humans.

Python and Django

Python is like my main language, using this technology I've made programs to monitor and control devices with the native TKinter library. I've use also DSP, Image processing, Matplotlib and others. Using Django I made my personal Blog and I'm in constantly learning.

C++ & Devices

I have strong bases in Microcontroller programming, I've used C++ in Arduino, Mbed, PSoC, PIC and STM boards, constantly i am oriented to create new devices in order to improve a life feature and also I've make applications for FPGA using VHDL language.

Electronics

I am an Electronic technologist from the Universidad Distrital FJDC in Bogotá - Colombia, there, I learned Analog and Digital electronic theory that I apply in all the aspects of my devices from the very first planning stage to the project management and creation.

Instrumentation

I love sensing the world, I know how to work the analog and digital sensors. I am passionate about sensors and actuators, with these components we can change the life of a disabled person, we can change the future of climate change and we can make it real all the ideas.
